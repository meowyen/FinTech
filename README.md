# Case Study: Square, Inc.

## Overview

[Square, Inc.](https://squareup.com/) is a software company founded in 2009 by Jack Dorsey (currently Twitter's CEO) and Jim McKelvey. Square was originally a mobile payments and merchant services company but has expanded into other domains such as food delivery, web hosting and digital banking. It is now a publicly traded company ([NYSE: SQ](https://www.nyse.com/quote/XNYS:SQ)) processing billions of dollars in payment transactions.

> The idea came about because co-founder Jim McKelvey was unable to accept credit card payment for his high-end glass faucets and fittings.
